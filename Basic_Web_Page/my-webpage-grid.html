Tshepiso Ramasenya

Bio

Tshepiso Ramasenya was born in Polokwane South Africa. He grew up there and subsequently moved to Pretoria. He obtained a Bachelors of Technology in Industrial Engineering at the University of Johannesburg and is currently participating in 4ir work-readiness programme. He has been described to have problem solving skills, entrepreneurial drive and a collaborative skills.

Origin: Polokwane

Polokwane ] meaning "Place of Safety" in Northern Sotho, also known by its former name, Pietersburg, is a city and the capital of the Limpopo Province of South Africa. It is South Africa's largest urban centre north of Gauteng.The city of Pietersburg was founded in the gold rush of the 1880's. The site of the city was initially owned by the Zuid Afrikaanse Volksraad who purchased the eastern half of a large farm known as Sterkloop in 1884. Pietersburg was named after a well known General, Petrus (Piet) Jacobus Joubert. On the 31st of July 1886 Landdros (Magistrate) Dietlof Siegfred Mare wrote his first official letter from the magistrates court and the town was officially recognized. The main street through the city centre is known as “ Landdros Mare Street” to this day.
